About the Project

What's This
Project About?

This project is part of the Modern Management course, where students are encouraged to learn real business management and develop effective selling strategies.

As part of this hands-on experience, our group created and sold homemade yogurt to primary school students.

Through this project, we gained practical knowledge in planning, marketing, teamwork, and business operations.

📋 Business Planning 🤝 Teamwork 💰 Cost Management 🛒 Customer Service 📊 Data Analysis

⚠️ Challenges

Problems & Solutions

⚠️ Problem 1
Product Melts Too Fast
Temperature control was difficult during transport and selling hours.
↓
✅ Solution
Used cooler boxes and improved storage to maintain product temperature longer.
⚠️ Problem 2
Insufficient Stock
Demand exceeded our production capacity on some days.
↓
✅ Solution
Increased production batches and planned ahead based on demand forecasting.
⚠️ Problem 3
Insufficient Refrigerator Space
Refrigerator space was not enough to store all yogurt stock properly.
↓
✅ Solution
Expanded refrigerator space to store more yogurt and maintain proper temperature.
